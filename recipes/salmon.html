Pan-fried salmon

Description

Have a perfectly cooked salmon fillet, complete with crisp skin, ready in under 10 minutes.

Serve with a side of buttery, seasonal green veg for a filling supper

Ingredients

  • 2 x 150g salmon fillets
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 20g unsalted butter
  • ½ lemon, juiced

Steps

  1. Generously season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Put the oil and butter in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat.
  3. Swirl the pan until melted and foaming and turn up the heat.
  4. Once the butter starts bubbling, add the salmon fillets to the pan, skin-side-down, and fry for 3 mins until crisp.
  5. Flip the fillets over, lower the heat and cook for 2 mins more, then drizzle with the lemon juice.
  6. Transfer the salmon to a plate and baste with any of the buttery juices left in the pan.
